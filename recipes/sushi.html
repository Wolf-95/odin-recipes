Sushis & Makis


An asian icon


A worldwide known and loved dish that merges deliciouness and healthiness


Ingredients


  • Rice and seaweed
  • Vinager with salt + sugar
  • Smoked salmon
  • Cheese + cucumbers

Steps


  1. Cook the rice and make mix it with the already done vinager
  2. Put rice on seaweed, put the desired fillings
  3. Roll and cut into 6-8 pieces
