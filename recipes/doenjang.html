Doenjang


This is a very popular Korean soup with meat


A popular meat soup made with a lot of vegetables and a red sauce. Sometimes it has a kimchi based broth to make it richer and with a lot more of flavour


Ingredients


  • Meat
  • Kimchi
  • Desired Vegetables
  • Broth

Steps


  1. Make the broth with the kimchi as a base
  2. Add the meat and vegetables
  3. Put spices and let it cook for a few hours on very low fire
