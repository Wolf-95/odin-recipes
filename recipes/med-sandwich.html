Mediterranean sandwich


A flavour packed mediterranean sandwich


This sandwich has flavours from the mediterranean culture, including vegetables like cherry tomatoes and garlic


Ingredients


  • Bread
  • Mustard & Mayonnaise
  • Smoked turkey
  • Cherry tomatoes

Steps


  1. Make the sauce with mustard & mayonnaise with persil and olive oil
  2. Golden cook the bread with butter
  3. Assemble everything
